TimeSynch

"TimeSynch, currently in development, is an application to help you let your contacts know if you are currently available or busy to receive calls, texts, facetime etc. We use an amalgamation of information to compute your status such as calendar meetings, availability hours as set by you, if you are driving etc. For people who travel a lot between various timezones, our app uses location services to update the current time of the user in real time; so your contacts know if it's too late for them to buzz you. "
